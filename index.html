Our Mission Statement

To empower adults with disabilities to become entrepreneurs within their communities.

Our Values

We are firm believers of inclusion, equality, and empowering individuals to have valued social roles within Irish society and within their communities. We do this by providing a person centered approach which is led by and tailored to the individual so that they can achieve their vision of success.

Our Approach

We want to offer individualised 1:1 supports to entrepreneurs. These supports will be physical, social and emotional. Each individual will be supported to nurture and grow their idea and will be given the tools to so in an empowering atmosphere.

Our Objective

To have a truly inclusive Ireland where individuals with disabilities can be empowered to become entrepreneurs.
